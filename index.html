Meme Coin Market Cap (MMC)

A decentralized, XRP-powered index of meme tokens

About the Project

Meme Coin Market Cap (MMC) is an XRP Ledger-powered project that tracks, indexes, and supports the growing ecosystem of XRP meme tokens. MMC creates a “basket” of curated meme coins, allocating XRP efficiently for community engagement, liquidity, and operational growth.

MMC’s mission is to highlight the lore, story, and culture behind XRP meme tokens while experimenting with DeFi-style allocation mechanics.

How It Works

  • Community-curated basket of XRP meme tokens.
  • Bot allocates 75% of incoming XRP to the basket.
  • 10% of inflows are burned to support scarcity.
  • 25% reserved for operational growth and project sustainability.

XRPL Alignment & Use Case

  • Experiments with DeFi-style token allocation on XRPL.
  • Supports onchain activity and engagement of XRP meme communities.
  • Acts as a proof-of-concept for future XRP DeFi indexes and automated distribution bots.
